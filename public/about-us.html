About US:

Yasan Hedayat is my wonderful three-year-old son, full of energy, curiosity, and heart. From the moment he wakes up, his enthusiasm for the world around him is contagious. Whether he's building with blocks, flipping through picture books, or exploring outside, Yasan is always eager to learn and play. His bright eyes and cheerful laughter bring so much joy to our home every day.

One of Yasan's favorite things to do is ask questions—lots of them! He's fascinated by animals, colors, and anything with wheels. He has a growing vocabulary and loves trying out new words, often surprising us with how much he remembers. Despite his young age, he already shows a gentle and caring nature, especially when he's around other kids or helping with small tasks at home.

Watching Yasan grow has been the most rewarding experience. Every day brings something new—a funny phrase, a new dance move, or a discovery that makes his face light up. I'm so proud of the kind, curious, and joyful little boy he's becoming, and I can't wait to see what the future holds for him.
